
Disney has connections to Ireland, as the Disney family is descended from Norman settlers in Munster.  

Was Walt Disney Irish? He visited Ireland in 1948 while working on *Darby O’Gill and the Little People*.  

Walt Disney’s father was of Irish heritage, descended from Irish Protestant families who had settled in Canada. Disney himself grew up with a sense of kinship to Ireland. Later in life, the Disney family even owned a castle in County Cork.  

Charlie Chaplin, on the other hand, visited the Butler Arms Hotel in Waterville annually for 10 years with his family, which became a well-known public event. The Chaplin family maintained a strong connection with the town and supported the 2019 Charlie Chaplin Festival held there, though the festival did not return after COVID-19.  

The festival was a major event for Waterville, and press photos captured the Chaplin family arriving in town. Despite the attention, they were mostly left alone to enjoy their holidays.  

By the end of that 10-year period, Chaplin was nearing 80 and in advancing years. By then, his family had settled in Switzerland, near Montreux.
