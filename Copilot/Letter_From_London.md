London Attractions:

Millennium Bridge: A walkway bridge across the Thames, leading to the Tate Modern and Shakespeare's Globe Theatre.

Southwark Cathedral: Home to Hodge, an absolute splendid gentleman.

The Kebab Shop: Unquestionably the best kebab shop in London, located on a street called 'The Cost.'

The Old Cheshire Cheese Pub: A historic pub on Fleet Street, frequented by Charles Dickens and Oliver Goldsmith, with an interesting story about a parish from 1929.
