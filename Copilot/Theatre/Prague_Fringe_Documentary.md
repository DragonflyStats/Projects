**Title**: *Prague Fringe: Six Days of Art, Stories, and Connections*

**Opening (5 minutes)**:  
- Montage of iconic Prague landmarks juxtaposed with festival scenes.  
- Narration sets the tone: The Prague Fringe Festival, six days of innovative performances in one of Europe’s most historic cities.  
- Brief introduction to the festival’s origins and its unique character, emphasizing the intimate settings and international appeal.  

**Act 1: The City as a Stage (15 minutes)**  
- Explore how Prague itself complements the festival.  
- Introduce key venues like hidden bars, small theatres, and cozy cafes that transform into performance spaces.  
- Interviews with local business owners, showing the symbiotic relationship between the festival and the community.  
- Footage of attendees navigating the city and stumbling upon unexpected performances.  

**Act 2: The Artists (20 minutes)**  
- Profile several performers from diverse backgrounds: emerging artists, established names, international acts.  
- Feature snippets of rehearsals, behind-the-scenes preparation, and intimate moments of creativity.  
- Personal interviews with the artists—what drew them to the festival, their inspirations, and how the Prague Fringe is different from other events.  

**Act 3: The Audience Experience (15 minutes)**  
- Capture the energy of the audience: their reactions, emotions, and post-show discussions.  
- Interviews with festival-goers who’ve come from near and far to experience the event.  
- Highlight moments of connection between artists and audiences.  

**Act 4: The Magic of Six Days (20 minutes)**  
- Show the progression of the festival through the six days: excitement of opening night, exhaustion yet joy mid-week, bittersweet closing performances.  
- Highlight moments of serendipity—unexpected encounters, impromptu collaborations, and standout performances.  
- Interviews reflecting on how the festival builds a temporary but vibrant community.  

**Conclusion (10 minutes)**  
- Montage of highlights from performances, audience interaction, and iconic Prague moments.  
- Reflection from artists, organizers, and attendees on the impact of the festival and its enduring legacy.  
- Narration closes with a statement of the festival's continued significance and growth.
